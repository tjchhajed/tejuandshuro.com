The Proposal


Having opposites interests, when they started sharing their hobbies with each other, teaching each other music, german language, basketball, football - the relationship between them started getting more than a friendship. As the time passed, Teju and Shuro started practicing the piano. The daily piano session used to be a much awaited moment for them. Becasuse this was the moment everyday when they used to see each other and after the practice they used to go for a walk. In an evening as such, Shuro finally proposed her. She was looking like the happiest person on earth that day. The proposal went like this -


What else do you like?

(His aim was to impress her. His expression was if he was going to bring what she likes from anywhere possible in the universe on her feet.)

I like eating

(As if she was starving since ages. This seems a complete lie when a thin person says this. But now she eats like a monster when shuro prepares some delicious food.)

Well, I like Cooking

(He just lied. He had never cooked before. But now he has become a pro chef after the commitment to keep his promise. Now he prepares delicious cuisines every now and then.)

So . . . ?

(She needn't have to proceed further continuing the discussion with a question. She did because she wanted shuro to propose her in his next words.)

So be my soulmate

(Please! Please! Please! I will cook, bake, brew anything that you like. It be Indian Curry, Chinese Noodles, German Maultasche, French Macaron, Italian Pasta, Tortellini, Lasgna. Gnocchi etc.)
